MY UNIQUE POEM

Souls,



Complex needs



interconnected



Light energy

binds them

  1. 2 hearts


  2. 2 minds


Become

  • 1 heart


  • 1 mind


its all in the

timing

the complexities

of a trick

  • Reciprocity

    with a kiss

    intuitive

    apport

    the exchange

    of a key,

    The Gift


    • MAGIC

    Metamorphosis

    The answer to this

    trick

    begins with the binding

    of his wrists.
